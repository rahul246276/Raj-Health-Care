What We Offer

Our Core Services

From trained nursing staff at your doorstep to medical equipment on rent — we handle everything so you can focus entirely on recovery

Home Nursing Care

Experienced male and female staff nurses deployed at your home for post-surgical recovery, chronic illness management, and round-the-clock patient supervision.

Learn more

Patient Attendants

Compassionate and trained patient attendants for in-home and hospital duty — providing hygiene assistance, feeding support, mobility help, and 24-hour companionship.

Learn more

Physiotherapy at Home

Certified physiotherapists visit your home to assist with post-operative rehabilitation, stroke recovery, orthopaedic conditions, and long-term mobility improvement.

Learn more

Hospital Beds on Rent

Hydraulic, motorised, and patient-care hospital beds available on rent or sale — home delivery with full setup included. Alfa beds and recliner mattresses also available.

Learn more

Respiratory Equipment

Ventilators, CPAP/BiPAP machines, oxygen cylinders, oxygen concentrators, nebulizers, and suction machines available on rent and sale with prompt home delivery.

Learn more

Mobility & Support Equipment

Wheelchairs, walkers, DVT pumps, over-bed tables, pulse oximeters, multipara monitors, and motorised recliners — everything needed for patient comfort and care at home.
