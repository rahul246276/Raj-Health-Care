Our Story

Care That Comes Straight From the Heart

Raj Nursing Bureau was established with one clear purpose: to ensure that no family ever has to struggle to find reliable, compassionate, and professional nursing care for their loved one. We began in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, and have since grown into a trusted name for home nursing, patient attendant services, physiotherapy, and medical equipment across all of India.

We understand that illness and recovery are not just medical challenges — they are deeply personal, emotional, and logistical ones too. That is why we take it upon ourselves to provide not just skilled staff, but the right people — those who bring warmth, patience, and genuine care alongside their professional expertise.

From a single nurse placed at a family's doorstep to managing complete home-care setups with equipment and staff — Raj Nursing Bureau handles it all, 24 hours a day, every day of the year.
