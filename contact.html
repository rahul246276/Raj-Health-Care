FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online via our website appointment form, call our helpline at +91 98765 43212, or visit the hospital reception in person. Online booking is quickest and available 24/7.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans including government and private schemes. Contact our billing team at +91 98765 43213 to verify your coverage before your visit.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring your government-issued ID, insurance card, previous medical records, a list of current medications, and any relevant test reports. We recommend arriving 15 minutes before your scheduled time.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes — our emergency department is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, staffed by experienced emergency physicians and nurses. Call +91 98765 43211 for immediate assistance or ambulance dispatch.

How can I access my test results?

Most results are ready within 24–48 hours. You may collect them from our lab reception or request delivery via email. For urgent results, please call our laboratory directly.

Can I get a second opinion from your doctors?

Absolutely. We encourage patients to seek second opinions. Book a consultation through our appointment system and bring all relevant medical records, scans, and reports from your previous doctors.
