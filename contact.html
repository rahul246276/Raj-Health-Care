Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you deploy a nurse or attendant?

In most cases we can deploy a nurse or patient attendant within 2–4 hours of your booking confirmation. For advance bookings, we match staff and confirm placement on the same day.

Do you provide services outside Noida?

Yes. Raj Nursing Bureau provides services all across India — not just in Noida and Delhi NCR. Call us with your location and we will confirm availability and make arrangements.

Are your nurses trained and verified?

Absolutely. Every nurse and attendant we deploy is medically qualified (GNM / B.Sc Nursing), registered with the nursing council, and has undergone a thorough background verification before being placed with any patient.

Can I rent medical equipment for just a few days?

Yes. We offer flexible rental periods — from a single day to several months. There is no minimum rental commitment. Call us to discuss rates for your specific equipment and duration.

Do you also sell medical equipment, or only rent?

We offer both options — rental and outright sale — for most of our medical equipment range including hospital beds, ventilators, oxygen concentrators, wheelchairs, and more.

What shift options are available for nursing staff?

We provide 12-hour day shift, 12-hour night shift, and full 24-hour duty options. Our team will discuss the most appropriate arrangement based on the patient's needs when you call.

Need Help Right Now?

Raj Nursing Bureau is available 24 hours a day. Do not hesitate — call us immediately for urgent nursing or equipment requirements.
