How We Screen Our Staff

We take quality and trust seriously — every staff member goes through a rigorous verification process before they care for your patient

ID & Document Verification

Government-issued ID, nursing council registration, and qualification certificates are verified before any placement.

Background Check

Every staff member undergoes a thorough background check to ensure complete safety and trustworthiness.

Skills Assessment

Clinical skills and practical nursing knowledge are assessed before we deploy any staff to a patient's home or hospital.

Performance Monitoring

We collect ongoing feedback from families to ensure consistently high standards of care and professionalism.
